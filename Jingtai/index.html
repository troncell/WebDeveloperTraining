Senses of life with lenses of design

A creative lab taking DESIGN as an integrative approach to mutually empower perceptions (wander) and interpretations (sign)
We believe in the creativity cultivated by interactions between individuals, which are the essential inspirations to make ‘Innovative Stuffs’ that are able to communicate with large varieties of humanity. We hold curiosity as the positive engine for exploration of endless possibilities of life, while keep it ordinary for the accumulation of every piece of effort.
